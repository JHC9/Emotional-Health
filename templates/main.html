How It Works

  • Sign up and provide your primary emergency contact.
  • Run the app in the background for 20 to 30 minutes while you engage in your activities.
  • View the generated bar chart and positivity scale for your emotions during the session.
  • Get a weekly score and personalized suggestions if needed.
